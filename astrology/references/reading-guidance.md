# Reading guidance — modern psychological astrology crib sheet

Distilled from chart2ai's `READING_STYLE_SECTIONS.modern` (`src/data/promptSections.ts`).
Use contemporary psychological astrology: emphasize personal development, archetypal
patterns, and conscious evolution. **Obey the Rule of Truth — only interpret data
printed in the chart report; never complete patterns from geometric assumptions.**

## Elements (triplicity)
- **Fire** (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) — action, enthusiasm, inspiration, identity
- **Earth** (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) — practicality, material focus, stability, resources
- **Air** (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) — communication, ideas, relationships, intellect
- **Water** (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) — emotion, intuition, depth, spirituality

## Modalities (quadruplicity)
- **Cardinal** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) — initiation, leadership, new beginnings
- **Fixed** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) — stability, persistence, resistance to change
- **Mutable** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) — adaptability, flexibility, completion

## Polarity
- **Masculine/positive/active** — Fire and Air (externally directed energy)
- **Feminine/negative/receptive** — Earth and Water (internally directed energy)

## Major aspects
- **Conjunction** (0°) — blending, unity, intensification
- **Opposition** (180°) — tension, awareness, projection, integration
- **Trine** (120°) — harmony, ease, natural flow, talents
- **Square** (90°) — conflict, dynamic tension, action required
- **Sextile** (60°) — opportunity, cooperation, potential
- **Quincunx/inconjunct** (150°) — adjustment, redirection, incompatibility

## Aspect patterns (only read patterns explicitly listed in the report)
- **T-Square** — dynamic tension → focused action. Apex planet = where growth pressure concentrates; the opposition is the fundamental polarity to integrate.
- **Grand Trine** — natural flow/harmony between three functions. Ease and talent, but complacency risk without conscious activation.
- **Stellium** — concentrated energy in one sign/house. Emphasis and specialization; blind spots in unoccupied areas.
- **Kite** — grand trine + opposition giving a focused outlet (the opposition point is the "release valve").
- **Yod (Finger of God)** — adjustment pattern, constant recalibration; apex planet integrates two incompatible energies.

## Dispositor trees
- **Final dispositors** = core psychological resources, ultimate sources of power.
- **Mutual reception cycles** = interdependent functions that must develop together (feedback loops).
- **Chain length** = complexity of integration required; long chains = multi-stage development.
- **Orphaned/self-disposed planets** = autonomous psychological drives.
- Trace flow from personal → social → outer planets to find developmental pathways. Outer-disposes-inner = collective influences on personal expression; inner-disposes-outer = personal mastery of collective energies.

## Synastry priorities (two charts)
In order of importance:
1. **Primary connections** (0.0–1.5° orb) — most significant dynamics.
2. **House overlays** — how each person activates the other's life areas.
3. **Composite/shared patterns** — relationship challenges and gifts.
4. **Secondary connections** (1.5–4.0° orb) — supporting themes.

Frame: planet-to-planet (energies interact), planet-to-angle (impact on identity/ASC and direction/MC), cross-chart patterns (shared curriculum), mutual reception (special bonds). Begin with tightest orbs, then layer overlays.

## Transit guidance
Only interpret transits printed in the report's transit-aspects section.
- **Primary** (0.0–1.5°) = peak activation; **secondary** (1.5–4.0°) = developing; **background** (4.0–6.0°) = subtle pressure.
- **Applying** = building energy; **separating** = integration.
- Duration by planet: outer (Uranus/Neptune/Pluto) = major restructuring; Saturn = structural lessons/testing; Jupiter = growth/expansion; inner = brief triggers.
- Assess the natal planet's condition (dignity/house/aspects) as the baseline for how the transit lands.

## Mandatory verification before detailed interpretation
- Check element + modality distribution for dominant themes.
- Identify stelliums / major aspect patterns first (only those explicitly listed).
- Verify rulership hierarchy (modern assignments); note the chart ruler early if clearly present.
- Note personal vs. social vs. generational planet emphasis.
- Cross-reference dignity conditions with house meanings.
- **Never complete a pattern from geometric assumptions — if it's not in the report, it doesn't exist.**
